Troubleshoot users' problems with MyBB

MyBB's Support Team and the Community are dedicated to helping users solve problems with their MyBB forums. If you are familiar with the software or enjoy troubleshooting, feel free to participate across our support channels.

Security Research

Help improve MyBB's security

The MyBB Team cooperates with security researchers on triage, resolution, analysis, and public disclosure of vulnerability information. If you're interested in web application security, have a look at our software to improve the safety of MyBB forums and earn recognition for successful findings.
