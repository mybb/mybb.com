Get in touch regarding the MyBB Project

All issues and feedback relating to the MyBB website and the community forums can be posted in the MyBB.com Community and Site Issues forum.

If you have an issue relating to your account on the Community Forums or other personal matters, please post in the Private Inquiries forum.

Alternatively, administrators can be reached via e-mail: contact@mybb.com.
